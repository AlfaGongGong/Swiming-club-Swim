ABOUT US

Meet our certified swimming instructors

Mateo

Click for bio

Mateo Todosijević, born in Sarajevo in 1999, is a passionate individual with a remarkable journey in the world of swimming. His story begins with the completion of his education at the Fifth Gymnasium, specializing in sports. During his third year of high school, he ventured to the USA, where he attended Dunellen High School, all the while maintaining an active involvement in the sport of swimming. It was during this time that he achieved notable accomplishments. In 2022, he achieved another milestone by earning his degree in Kinesiology, solidifying his knowledge in the field of human movement and exercise. Mateo's coaching journey began in 2017, driven by a desire to share his extensive swimming expertise with aspiring athletes. In 2023, he established his very own club, "Swim," with the mission of fostering a community of passionate swimmers. Notably, in the same year, Mateo obtained his coaching license from FINA, the international governing body of swimming, a testament to his commitment to coaching excellence. Mateo's vision is to pass on his wealth of swimming experience to younger generations, ensuring that the love for the sport continues to flourish.

Mak

Click for bio

Mak Oručević, a passionate swimmer with over a decade of experience in the sport. For the past two years, he has been dedicated to helping children and youth develop their swimming skills as a certified swimming instructor. Additionally, he holds a lifeguard certification from the International Life Saving Federation (ILS), making him a trusted guardian in both open and closed waters.

Welcome to Swim, your gateway to the world of swimming. Our mission is to foster a lifelong love for the water and provide the best swimming experiences. We are passionate about what we do, and we're committed to helping you achieve your swimming goals. At Swim, we believe that swimming is more than just a skill; it's a journey that instills confidence, discipline, and a deep appreciation for the water. Our club was founded in 2023 with this vision in mind. We take pride in our team of dedicated and experienced instructors who are all certified by FINA, the international governing body of swimming. They are here to guide you, support your progress, and ensure that you receive the highest quality of instruction. Our programs cater to swimmers of all levels, from beginners taking their first strokes to advanced swimmers looking to refine their techniques and achieve competitive goals. Whether you're a child, a teenager, or an adult, we offer tailored programs that suit your needs and abilities. Join us at Swim and become a part of our passionate and growing swimming community. Let us help you embark on a journey of self-improvement, fitness, and the sheer joy of being in the water. Dive into your swimming adventure with us, and together, we'll make a splash!
